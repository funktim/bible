Blessed

Μακάριοι (Makarioi)
Adjective - Nominative Masculine Plural
Strong's Greek 3107: Happy, blessed, to be envied. A prolonged form of the poetical makar; supremely blest; by extension, fortunate, well off.

Wiktionary
From μάκαρ (mákar, “blessed”) +‎ -ιος (-ios, adjective suffix), of unknown, apparently pre-Greek, origin.
(mostly of men) blessed, happy
